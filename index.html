Find your things

Misplacing something like your wallet doesn’t have to be a big deal when it has a HashTag attached. Just tap “Play Sound” in the Hash app and the HashTag on your keys will ring. Tap on "Find" and follow the directions until you are right next to the HashTag.

Find your Phone

Lost your phone? Make your phone ring by using HashTag, like the ones on your keys or wallet. Press the HashTag button twice. It works even if your phone is on silent.

Hash Alerts

These alerts make sure you don’t leave anything behind, no matter where you are or where you move. We will make sure that you leave that location with all of the HashTaged items you brought with you.

Location History

If you lose something when you're out and about, HashTag can help you locate it. View its most recent location on maps. It'll show you the last time when it was with you and also you can see the last 30 days location history.

Lost and Found

When you lose your HashTag, enable Lost Mode in the HashTag app and leave a message with a phone number. When someone finds it, they can get your contact info by tapping your HashTag with an NFC-capable smartphone. In addition, whenever any HashTag app detects it, you'll receive an automatic notification of its location!
