Find your things

Misplacing something like your wallet doesn’t have to be a big deal when it has HashTag attached. Just tap “Play Sound” in the Hash app and the HashTag on your keys will ring. Follow the direction after tapping “Find” and you reach next to the HashTag.

Find your Phone

Lost your phone? Use HashTag, like the ones on your keys or wallet, to make your phone ring. Just double press the HashTag logo button. It works even if your phone is on silent.

Smart Alerts

These alerts make sure you don’t leave anything behind, no matter where you are or where you go. We’ll check to make sure you leave that location with all of the Hashed items you first arrived with.

Location History

If you lose something when you’re out and about, HashTag can help you locate it. View its most recent location on a map. It’ll show you the last time it was with you and also you can see the last 30 days history.

Lost and Found

If you lose your HashTag then enable ‘Lost Mode’ in Hash app and leave a number with message. When someone finds it, they can get your contact info by tapping your HashTag with an NFC-capable smartphone. And also when it’s detected by any Hash device in the network, you’ll automatically get a notification.
